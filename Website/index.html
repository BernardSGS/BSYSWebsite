Meet BSYS

We're a Concordia-based student group dedicated to going beyond the curriculum and leaping into the future of technological input: biosignals, from accelerometery to bioelectricity and beyond. Come join us at the District 3 Innovation Center as we study and experiment with the latest wearables and brain-computer interfaces. No experience necessary: let's learn together!

Join us

Have a look at our Events and feel free to joins as we learn. Thanks to District 3 Innovation Center, NeurotechX and our sponsors, we will have the chance to work with the latest softwares and hardwares such as the Muse HeadBand and the Open BCI.
